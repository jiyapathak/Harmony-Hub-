About Test Intern

Building the future of internship platforms and professional development

Who We Are

Test Intern is a leading internship platform dedicated to connecting talented students and young professionals with meaningful opportunities at innovative companies. We believe in the power of practical experience and mentorship to shape the next generation of leaders.

Founded in 2023, our mission has been to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world professional experience. We work with hundreds of companies across various industries to provide internships that matter.
